I, Bramanta Wijaya, is someone who follow my heart because I know that I am the only one who understand the rush of passion that runs within it. I knew it ever since I was a child. Back then, I used to spend my time drawing, from faces to dresses. Mostly dresses, though. I was in my own world that made me happy. I was dreaming that one day, these drawings would come true. I was dreaming that they will be worn by the women who are not afraid to show their true colours. I was dreaming that I could share the happiness I felt, the passion I’ve always had.

I believe that love is simply love. Love is between two people who live in each other all along, the souls that become one. For this, I created Sposa. For every woman who believes in her true self, for every woman who found her true love. For a wedding, once and the one that lasts forever.

A confident and happy bride is one that feels like herself. The wedding dress I design is made to showcase your true self so that you will be the most beautiful and prideful version of yourself. Because a wedding is the moment where you can be your true self and the dress is the way through which you can express your charm and essence. For this – personality is the key. Each detail is carefully curated to meet your needs and personality. Pockets, customized lace, and shapes to attune to your body are essential. Because I believe that it is your day and my designs strive to celebrate that fact.

Carefree, joy, but still looks feminine. You’re a simple woman who puts a soft swipe of peach blush and a sheer hint of lipstick. You know you don’t need too much make-up to cover your soul. Indeed, the make-up you wear is the reflection of your original personality. For this, I’d love to highlight both your inner and outer character in you.

Pink Label reflects the personality that you wear in day-to-day life. With a variety of people that you meet, it can be a rollercoaster of emotion. Through pink label, I design versatile outfit that does not weigh you down. It’s lightweight and it makes you free to jump between this and that. It’s easy to wear, it’s your companion to flee between the days.

Intimate by Bramanta Wijaya is intimacy reinterpreted to radiates women’s natural allure. As lace and delicate textured fab- rics are the main material in cre- ating these robes, Bramanta Wijaya envision of creating attire you can rent for any intimate moment from being a bride, a mother to – be, or for photo shoots (exclu- sive lingerie and attire).

Something that’s versatile for your moments and intimately show- ing the allure of your body. The word “IM” in Intimate is a representation of the “I’m” or “me” in every woman. As it follows the flow of your body, the silhoutte will reveal the true you. I’m Intimate
